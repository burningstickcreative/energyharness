Site Assessment

The first step of a proper lighting upgrade is the site assessment where we tour your facility to determine all the options available to you. This is when you explain your vision, your needs, and we determine what is needed to make it possible.

After finishing the walkthrough of your facility, our team of lighting experts then sets to work on building a proposal. This is where we can explain exactly what kind of light you need and show you how much money you will save!

Our staff of expert lighting specialists is always eager to tackle its next project. Call today to schedule your free site assessment!
